Greeting From the Embassy

It is our pleasure to welcome you to this website which depicts the Embassy’s activities in Canada and shares pertinent information for travelers and investors to Zimbabwe. Diplomatic relations between Zimbabwe and Canada date back to 1980 when Zimbabwe attained independence.The Mission was established to execute the mandate of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs which is to promote the political and economic interests, image and influence of the Republic of Zimbabwe in the international community and to protect the interests of Zimbabweans abroad.Look around and if you have any questions or concerns feel free to get in touch, our embassy staff are always happy to help.
