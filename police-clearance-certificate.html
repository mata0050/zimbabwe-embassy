IMPORTANT!

‍

  1. Due to corona virus outbreak, the embassy is suspending All consular services until further notice.
  2. The Government of Zimbabwe continues to adhere to and uphold World Health Organization regulations, guidelines, advisories on the travel requirements. All travellers and visitors into the country including those from countries with confirmed COVID-19 will be screened at ports of entry into Zimbabwe. However, travellers with signs and symptoms of the virus will be referred immediately to designated isolation centres for Further clinical examination and assistance.
