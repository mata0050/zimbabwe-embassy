Press Statement on the exictence of a web-base E - VISA Platform

‍

Warning against use of parallel web-based e-visa platform

It has come to the attention of the Department of Immigration that many Zimbabweans based outside the county are being duped by a false web-based e-visa platform when they tender their online visa applications.

This parallel website which appears as www.zimbabwevisa.com is not an authentic Zimbabwe Immigration website. The parallel web-based e-visa platform was unearthed folling numerous complaints of delays by viss applicants.

One of the characteristics of parallel platform is that it bears a disclaimer clause that it has no affiliation with the Government of Zimbabwe but it utilizes the Zimbabwe Government "coat of arms". Another feature of the fake website is that it does not generate an e-visa tracking number. In addition, its domain name bears a striking and misleading resemblance to the Zimbabwe Immigration website.

The public is hereby warned to desist from using www.zimbabwevisa.com when applying for Zimbabwe visas. Applications for visa to enter Zimbabwe must be done only on the official website www.evisa.gov.zw.

The Ministry of Foreign Affairs and International Trade wishes to advise the general public especially diaspotans to take serious heed of this warning.

‍

C.Mutodza

Deputy Director: Legal & Consular Affairs

Visas

In order to visit Zimbabwe you may need to apply for a visa. Your nationality will determine which of 3 visa requirement categories you fall into. Your category will tell you if you need to apply for a visa and when:

In order to determine your category please consult the Zimbabwe immigration website.

Category A

No visa required.

Category B

Visa on arrival at the port of entry.

Category C

Visa before arrival.

With effect from 1st November 2019, tourist visas for Canadian Passport holders shall be issued at the port of entry into Zimbabwe. Kindly note that visa applications will no longer be processed by the Embassy. The Embassy will continue issuing visas for holders of Canadian Diplomatic and official passports.

To apply for a visa visit:‍

www.evisa.gov.zw‍
